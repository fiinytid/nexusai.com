How It Works

3 Simple Steps

Setup once, keep building forever.

1
Login & Setup
Sign in with Google + Roblox. Download the NEXUS AI plugin for Roblox Studio from our Discord.
2
Connect Plugin
Open Roblox Studio, click NEXUS AI in the toolbar, and hit CONNECT. Green light = ready to go!
3
Chat & Build
Describe what you want. AI immediately injects code + objects into your place. That's it!

}
